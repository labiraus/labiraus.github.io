*“my child’s confidence has grown from meeting new people at groups. She was clingy around people to start…before attending groups”*

* Focus on the encouragement of positive interaction between parent & child through quality play activities.
* Focus on healthy eating and physical activity.
* Free healthy breakfast for children.
* Opportunity for parents meet others and develop social networks.
* Workshops on health, parenting skills, child safety etc.
* Safe place for parents to talk and share concerns – One to One confidential support if required.
