## Need Support ?

*"If you are a parent with at least one child under the age of five then Home-Start Nottingham may be able to help you."*

We will try to support any family that needs us, as long as they have at least one child under five.

Support is usually provided by volunteers who visit you in your own home for approximately 2 hours a week to offer support, friendship and practical assistance.

The best way to find out what support from Home-Start Nottingham really means is to read the comments from families quoted throughout this site.

## Please Note

Home-Start Nottingham volunteers are not home helps, do not provide a babysitting service and cannot take children to school on a regular basis, although practical help may often be part of a visit.

## What do I do next?

You can contact Home-Start Nottingham directly on 0115 962 4262 and a co-ordinator will arrange to visit you at home to discuss your needs and decide whether Home-Start Nottingham is appropriate for you.

You can contact your health visitor, social worker or any other agency you are involved with and ask them to make the referral for you.
