Story and Rhyme time groups

These groups are for children between 0-4 years old. It is specifically designed to develop children’s learning and communication skills. Together you will enjoy learning new songs, dancing, reading and making new friends. This is an avenue for children to develop their social skills. The group is delivered within the library network at Strelley, Clifton, St Anns, and Hyson Green.
